About Us

AMPM Hair Studio and Academy is the leading premier chain of salons with 4 branches and 1 academy in Klang, boasting over 10 years of expertise in hairdressing and education. At AMPM Hair Studio and Academy, our education team provides comprehensive, innovative training through various professional courses for aspiring talents aiming to excel in the hair and beauty industry. We are dedicated to inspiring others to pursue their passion and have successfully trained numerous talents to master the latest trends and techniques in this dynamic field, many of whom have become successful hairdressers and salon owners. Our experience and reputation have also afforded us and our students opportunities to collaborate as hairdressing partners for various events.
